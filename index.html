01 // ABOUT US

Welcome to INSPRANO 2K25: Where Innovation Meets Transformation

Insprano, the annual techfest of GCEK Bhawanipatna, proudly presents Robosprano—a spectacular showcase dedicated solely to the world of robotics. Hosted by the illustrious Kilobots robotics club, Robosprano is where innovation meets excitement. This vibrant arena provides an unparalleled platform for budding engineers and tech enthusiasts to demonstrate their ingenuity and expertise.

With a range of thrilling competitions and challenges, Robosprano pushes the boundaries of modern robotics, fostering a spirit of creativity, teamwork, and relentless pursuit of excellence. Dive into the future of technology at Insprano’s Robosprano and witness the marvels of human and machine collaboration in a festival like no other.

Together, let's drive the future of technology forward !!

03// Events

i // Death Race

(i) Race to Survive. Fight to Win. Live for the Thrill.

Death Race is an exhilarating competition that tests pure knowledge of robotics and automation. The goal is to navigate the track as quickly and accurately as possible, while overcoming obstacles, following lines and responding to environmental challenges. Speed and strategy are key in robot racing, where every millisecond counts. Let’s fuel the passion for speed and dive into a pool of this magnificent competition.

02 // Robo Sumo

(II) Precision. Power. Domination

Witness the intensity of the Robo Sumo, where two teams battle head- to-head to push their opponent's bot out of the ring. Teams control their customized sumo bots manually, using either wired or wireless methods, in a fierce showdown of strategy and strength. The first team to successfully knock the other's bot off the ring earns a point, bringing them closer to victory. Get ready for a compact yet thrilling clash in the world of Robo Sumo!

Rule Book Register

03 // Line Follower

(III) Path to Precision: Navigate with Excellence

Line follower is a dynamic and engaging competition that blends hardware design, software development, and problem-solving skills. Participants must design an autonomous robot that can detect the given track using sensors and reach the finishing point in the least time.It tests the participants' ability to develop algorithms for controlling the robot’s movement, detecting the line, and responding to changes in the track layout.It is definitely for someone willing to ace in the field of automated systems.

Rule Book Register

04 // Robo Soccer

(IV) Unleash the Future: Score Big with Robo Soccer

Witness the intensity of Robo Soccer, where two teams battle head to head to goal in their opponents goalpost. Teams control their customized soccer bots manually, using either wired or wireless methods, in a fierce showdown of strategy and strength. The team which scores highest number of goals in between the given time will win the game. Get ready for a compact yet thrilling clash in the world of Robo soccer.
